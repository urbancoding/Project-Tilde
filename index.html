Mission

Online communication lacks a meaningful expression for sarcasm, mischievousness, and epic wins. So we're changing the English language to make one~

Grammar

The awesome character (~) may be used after a word or phrase to emphasize sweetness, savagery, or excitedness. When ending a sentence, it takes the place of a period. The tilde may not be used to precede a word or for the generally mundane.
